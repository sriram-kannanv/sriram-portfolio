About me

Hi, I’m Sriram K, a sophomore pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems at Vel Tech Multi Tech Dr. Rangarajan Dr. Sakunthala Engineering College (Autonomous) in Chennai, India. I’ve always been curious about how technology shapes the world, and this curiosity drives me to dive deeper into coding and problem-solving every day.

I’m proud to have earned an NPTEL certification in Programming in Java from IIT Kharagpur, achieving the Elite rank. It was a rewarding experience that not only strengthened my technical foundation but also fueled my passion for innovation. Currently, I’m honing my skills in full-stack development, with a goal to create applications that truly make a difference.

Beyond coding, I’m a person who loves to explore different dimensions of life. I’m an avid reader, always looking for stories or ideas that inspire me. Writing is another passion of mine, and I run a blog called Optimistishere on WordPress, where I share my thoughts to spread positivity. Traveling is close to my heart—it gives me fresh perspectives and fuels my creativity.

In a nutshell, I’d describe myself as a traveler, coder, writer, and most importantly, an optimist who believes in embracing challenges and growing from them. As I continue my journey, I’m excited about the opportunities to learn, create, and contribute meaningfully to the world.
